Classic Lasagna Recipe



Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef (sausage or mix)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley (or 1 tbsp dried parsley)
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt & 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the Noodles

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles and cook according to the package directions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce

    In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes until softened.
    Stir in the marinara sauce and tomato sauce. Add the oregano, salt, and pepper. Let simmer for 10 minutes to blend the flavors.

  3. Make the Cheese Filling

    In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese, and parsley. Stir until well mixed.

  4. Assemble the Lasagna

    Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
    Add a layer of lasagna noodles (about 3 to 4 noodles, depending on the size of your dish).
    Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    Top with a layer of meat sauce, followed by a handful of shredded mozzarella and Parmesan.
    Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce and cheese on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna

    Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden.
    Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

  6. Serve and Enjoy!

    Serve the lasagna with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal!
